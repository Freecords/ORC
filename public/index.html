Key Features

Immutable

Once issued, the ORC cannot change

Globally Unique

Combines issuer, time, and randomness

Decentralized

Any approved issuer can generate ORCs

Compact

Short enough for APIs, URLs, and metadata

Traceable

Can identify platform, year, and source

Open Standard

Free to use and implement

Building the Future Together
